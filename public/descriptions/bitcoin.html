Bitcoin is the first and most well-known cryptocurrency, created in 2009 by the pseudonymous Satoshi Nakamoto. It operates on a decentralized peer-to-peer network without the need for intermediaries.

Bitcoin blockchain is only used to store and transfer Bitcoin and doesn't have an execution layer.

It creates two issues:

  • It's not possible to build DeFi on Bitcoin
  • It's not possible to build a trully decentralized bridge on Bitcoin

Thus, we have a lot of various BTC flavors on different blockchains.

Providing an overview of all of those BTC flavors is a puprose of this map.
