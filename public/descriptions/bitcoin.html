Bitcoin (BTC)

Bitcoin is the first and most well-known cryptocurrency, created in 2009 by the pseudonymous Satoshi Nakamoto. It operates on a decentralized peer-to-peer network without the need for intermediaries.

Key Features:
  • Decentralized: No single entity controls the Bitcoin network
  • Limited Supply: Maximum of 21 million BTC will ever exist
  • Proof of Work: Uses computational power to secure the network
  • Transparent: All transactions are recorded on a public blockchain
Current Status:

Bitcoin serves as the foundation for the entire cryptocurrency ecosystem and is often referred to as "digital gold" due to its store of value properties.

Network: Bitcoin Mainnet
Block Time: ~10 minutes
Consensus: Proof of Work
