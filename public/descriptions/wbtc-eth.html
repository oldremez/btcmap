Wrapped Bitcoin (WBTC)

WBTC is an ERC-20 token that represents Bitcoin on the Ethereum blockchain. It allows Bitcoin holders to participate in Ethereum's DeFi ecosystem while maintaining exposure to Bitcoin's price movements.

How it Works:
  • 1:1 Backing: Each WBTC token is backed by 1 BTC held in custody
  • BitGo Custody: Bitcoin is held securely by BitGo, a regulated custodian
  • Ethereum Compatibility: Can be used in any ERC-20 compatible application
  • Redeemable: WBTC can be burned to redeem the underlying BTC
Use Cases:

WBTC enables Bitcoin holders to access Ethereum's DeFi protocols for lending, borrowing, yield farming, and trading without selling their Bitcoin position.
