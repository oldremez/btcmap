SolvBTC is a liquidity aggregator for BTC.

The idea is to wrap most of the known BTC falvors into a single token.

SolvBTC exists only on EVM chains currently.

SolvBTC can be minted from:

  • BTCB on BSC
  • WBTC on Ethereum, Arbitrum, BOB, Linea, Soneium, Berachain
  • FBTC on Ethereum, Mantle
  • BTC.b on Avalanche
  • tBTC on Base
  • cbBTC on Base
  • RBTC on Rootstock
  • KBTC on Ink
  • UBTC on HyperEVM

Canonical WBTC is minted on the same blockchains:

  • Ethereum
  • BSC
  • Arbitrum
  • Base
  • BOB
  • Avalanche
  • Berachain
  • HyperEVM
  • Ink
  • Rootstock
