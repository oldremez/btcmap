allBTC means alloyed BTC on Osmosis.

Basically, allBTC is a wrapper around different BTC flavors available on Osmosis:

  • Canonical WBTC
  • WBTC bridged from Ethereum with Axelar
  • WBTC bridged from Ethereum with Eureka
  • cbBTC bridged from Ethereum with Axelar
  • ckBTC
  • BTC.int3
  • nBTC

The assets are wrapped with a "7 Token Pool" on Osmosis, which allows to "swap" between the underlying assets to allBTC and vice-versa.
